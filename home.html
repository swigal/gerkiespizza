About Gerkie’s

The story behind the pop‑up and the pies.

Our Story

Gerkie’s started with a simple idea: great pizza shouldn’t stay in one place. We built a pop‑up that brings the fire wherever the people are—breweries, markets, block parties, and beyond.

Every pie starts with dough that rests for days, not hours. We layer on fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a little bit of chaos from a hot oven. The result? Pizza worth chasing.

What Makes Us Different

  • Hand‑crafted dough and fresh ingredients
  • Rotating specials and limited‑run pies
  • Pop‑up spirit—always on the move
  • Community‑driven, people‑first energy

FAQ

How far do you travel?

We typically serve within a set radius of our home base, but reach out and we’ll see what’s possible.

Do you cater private events?

Yes—birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and more. Use the form to tell us about your plans.

Do you offer gluten‑free options?

We can offer limited gluten‑friendly options at select pop‑ups. Ask about availability when you book.

Can you handle large crowds?

We’re built for busy nights and hungry lines. Let us know your expected headcount and timing.
